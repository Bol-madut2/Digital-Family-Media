About Digital Family Media

Our story, mission, and the team behind your family's digital solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by a group of parents and tech enthusiasts, Digital Family Media began with a simple idea: technology should bring families together, not pull them apart.

What started as a small project to create better parental controls has grown into a full-service digital media company focused on family-friendly solutions.
